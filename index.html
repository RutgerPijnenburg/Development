Goals

For this year I had several goals and I made a lot of progress towards them. My first goal was to improve my communication skills, my plan was to do this by going outside of my comfort zone, to join an association and join activities regularly, and I followed this through, I joined Kinjin, where I joined an activity once a week and this helped me in improving communication, it gave me the chance to talk to new people on a weekly basis and gave me the opportunity to show who I was.
Another of my goals was to improve in Blender, I had planned to do this by 1, using Blender 3 times a week (30 min sessions) and joining competitions, I somewhat managed to do this first goal, doing way more at times, but none at others, but this second part I did not do at all, this was mostly because of procrastination, therefore I will set a timeframe this time, in one month from writing this I will have found a competition I will enter. I improved a lot in Blender, even though I did not follow my goals to a T, however, I did find that I really cannot trust on school taking me where I want to be, therefore I will have to do more in my own, free time.
My third goal was to get better at prototyping and I did not set SMART goals, and therefore I had trouble improving, so for next year I make it SMART, I will do one prototype per week.
